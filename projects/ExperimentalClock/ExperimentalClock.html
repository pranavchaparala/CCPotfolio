EXPERIMENTAL CLOCK

DESCRIPTION

Addressing the myth around death being predetermined by the number of breaths. This experimental clock measures the passage of time based on the user's mood and breath rate.

When the user is happier times seem to pass faster, making their breath rate tends to increase. Conversely, when the user is sadder, their breath rate may slow down, creating a perception of time moving more slowly.

By connecting the subjective experience of time with a fundamental biological process, this clock offers a unique and personalized way to measure the passage of time.
